Flower Breeze

Flowebreeze has rebranded this FW 21 season as a contemporary high-end casual wear.
We are bringing the three disconnected identities of Flower, Breeze and Ninny into one unified identity under the theme of “effortless chic.”
We have focused on creating high quality items with a unique edge that you can wear effortlessly yet still be stylish and chic.
Furthermore, this season we are presenting a special collaboration line of casual wear with style icon Jung-won Cha.
